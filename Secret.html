When the crystal vaults of heaven open over the mortal earth, and the Great Cult awakens its Dark Ruler from the centuries-old sleep, the gates of eternal chaos will descend upon the world.

Only a Cursed Son, born of the blood of an ancient family, will take the seal of oblivion in his hands. But what price would a mortal who colluded with the darkness itself pay?

The blood of the stars will be shed on the altar of eternity when the chosen one steps onto the path paved with the bones of his ancestors. Saving the world will turn into eternal damnation for anyone who dares to touch a secret that is older than death itself.

Time is counting back, and the sands of eternity are already flowing through the fingers of the gods...
